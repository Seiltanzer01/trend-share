About Us

Uncle John DAO is a decentralized autonomous organization managing the UJO meme coin. We create a community focused on fun and active participation of every member. Our Web3 application is an innovative SocialFi project that brings together traders to collaboratively study the market and identify its inefficiencies. The system of interactive voting and forecasting helps analyze market movements, share the best setups, and form collective intelligence for more accurate price predictions. Integrated into the ecosystem is the AI "Uncle John," which analyzes your trade journal, identifies mistakes, and suggests ways to correct them, making your trading as efficient as possible. This is complemented by a trained neural network that provides trend forecasts and key market levels. Activity on the platform is rewarded with tokens, premium access, and unique analytical data. Professional traders monetize their strategies by earning through subscriptions and attracting new participants. Blockchain integration, automatic payouts, and powerful analytics tools make the application the ideal assistant for every trader, turning trading into a team-oriented, cohesive, and profitable activity.
